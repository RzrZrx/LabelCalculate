🧮 Labels per Roll Calculator
How to use
  1. Enter the Core Inner Diameter (mm) — the inner bore of the cardboard or plastic core the labels are wound on. Common sizes: 25 mm (1″), 38 mm (1.5″), 76 mm (3″).
  2. Enter the Roll Outer Diameter (mm) — the full diameter of the wound roll including all the label material.
  3. Enter the Label Repeat (mm) — the distance from the top of one label to the top of the next, including the gap between labels.
  4. Enter the Material Thickness (µm) — the combined thickness of the label face stock plus the liner. Use the preset dropdown for common label materials, or enter your own value.
  5. The calculator will instantly show the approximate number of labels per roll along with the estimated roll length in metres.
